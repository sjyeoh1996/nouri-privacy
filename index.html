🌿 Nouri — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Data We Collect

Nouri collects your email address (for login), food logs, blood sugar readings, and medication records. All data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide Nouri app features. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers with encryption at rest and in transit.

AI Features

When you use Scan Meal or T2D Coach, anonymised meal descriptions may be sent to OpenAI's API for processing. No personally identifiable information is included.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Medical Disclaimer

Nouri is a personal health tracking tool, not a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
